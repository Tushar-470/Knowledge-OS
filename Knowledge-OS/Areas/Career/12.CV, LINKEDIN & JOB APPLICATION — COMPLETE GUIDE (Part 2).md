### 🔧 Customization Checklist (USA)

- [ ] Find the specific Job ID from the job posting

### Cover Letter DO's and DON'Ts

  

| ✅ DO | ❌ DON'T |

|:---|:---|

| Address a specific person if possible (check LinkedIn) | Use "To Whom It May Concern" |

| Research 1 specific fact about the company | Write generic letters applicable to any company |

| Lead with YOUR value, not your need ("I can contribute…") | Lead with "I need a job" / "I want to learn" |

| Match keywords from the job posting | Use different terminology than the JD |

| Keep it to 1 page | Write 2+ pages of text |

| Proofread 3 times | Submit with typos — instant rejection |

| Include your LinkedIn URL | Forget digital presence |

| End with a call to action ("I welcome the opportunity to discuss…") | End with "Thanking you in anticipation" (outdated) |

## 6-MONTH JOB SEARCH TIMELINE

  

| Month | Focus | Target |

|:---|:---|:---|

| **Month 1** | Preparation | Complete all study documents; build CV (3 versions); optimize LinkedIn; list 50 target companies |

| **Month 2** | Apply — Wave 1 | Apply to 20 companies (India focus); start connecting on LinkedIn (50 connections/week); attend 1 pharma job fair/webinar |

| **Month 3** | Apply — Wave 2 + Interviews | Apply to 20 more companies (include USA/EU); begin mock interviews; attend any campus placements |

| **Month 4** | Active Interviewing | Focus on interview preparation; follow up on all applications; network aggressively on LinkedIn |

| **Month 5** | Offers & Negotiation | Evaluate offers using comparison matrix; negotiate salary/benefits; make decision |

| **Month 6** | Joining & Onboarding | Complete joining formalities; begin reading company SOPs; start any pre-joining certifications |

> [!TIP]

> **The #1 Job Search Strategy That Most Freshers Ignore:**

>

> **Employee referrals have a 10× higher conversion rate than online applications.** Instead of ONLY applying online, find someone who works at your target company (LinkedIn Alumni tool or mutual connections) and ask them to refer you internally. Most pharma companies have a referral bonus program (₹10K–₹50K), so the employee is incentivized to help you.

>

> Your approach: Apply online (for ATS tracking) + simultaneously message someone internally for a referral. This dual approach dramatically increases your chances.
