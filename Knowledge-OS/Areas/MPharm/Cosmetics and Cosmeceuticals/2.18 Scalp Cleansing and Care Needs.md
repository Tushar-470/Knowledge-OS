# Scalp: Cleansing and Care Needs (10 Marks)

## Introduction

The **scalp** is a specialized skin area containing a large number of **hair follicles, sebaceous glands, and sweat glands**. It produces sebum that lubricates hair and scalp. Improper scalp care can lead to **dandruff, seborrhea, itching, scalp infections, and hair loss**. Therefore, proper cleansing and care are essential for maintaining healthy hair and scalp.

---

# Characteristics of Scalp

- Highest density of hair follicles.
    
- Rich in sebaceous glands.
    
- Produces sebum continuously.
    
- Warm and humid environment favors microbial growth.
    
- Prone to dandruff and hair fall.
    

---

# Cleansing Needs of Scalp

## 1. Removal of Excess Sebum

- Sebaceous glands produce large amounts of sebum.
    
- Excess sebum causes oily scalp and dandruff.
    

## 2. Removal of Dirt and Pollutants

- Dust and environmental pollutants accumulate on scalp and hair.
    
- Regular cleansing maintains scalp hygiene.
    

## 3. Removal of Dead Cells

- Prevents dandruff formation and follicular blockage.
    

## 4. Control of Microorganisms

- Reduces growth of **Malassezia** species associated with dandruff.
    

### Ideal Cleansers

- Mild shampoos with pH **5.0–5.5**
    
- Anti-dandruff shampoos when necessary
    

---

# Care Needs of Scalp

## 1. Dandruff Control

### Cause

- Excess sebum
    
- Growth of _Malassezia_ fungi
    

### Ingredients

- Zinc Pyrithione
    
- Ketoconazole
    
- Piroctone Olamine
    

---

## 2. Hair Growth Support

Healthy scalp promotes healthy hair growth.

### Ingredients

- Minoxidil
    
- Caffeine
    
- Peptides
    

---

## 3. Moisturization

- Prevents scalp dryness and itching.
    
- Maintains scalp barrier function.
    

### Ingredients

- Aloe vera
    
- Panthenol
    
- Glycerin
    

---

## 4. Prevention of Hair Loss

### Causes

- Poor scalp health
    
- Inflammation
    
- Excess sebum
    

### Care

- Regular cleansing
    
- Scalp massage
    
- Use of appropriate hair growth products
    

---

## 5. Conditioning of Hair

- Conditioners improve hair softness and manageability.
    
- Usually applied to hair shaft rather than scalp.
    

---

# Common Scalp Problems

|Problem|Cause|
|---|---|
|Dandruff|Malassezia growth|
|Oily scalp|Excess sebum|
|Dry scalp|Lack of moisture|
|Itching|Irritation and inflammation|
|Hair loss|Follicular dysfunction|

# Conclusion

The scalp requires **regular cleansing, dandruff control, moisturization, and maintenance of follicular health**. Proper use of pH-balanced shampoos and scalp care products helps maintain a healthy scalp environment and promotes healthy hair growth.
