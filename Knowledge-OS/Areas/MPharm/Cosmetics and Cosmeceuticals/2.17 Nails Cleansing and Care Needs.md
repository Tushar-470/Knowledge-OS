# Nails: Cleansing and Care Needs (10 Marks)

## Introduction

Nails are hard keratinized structures that protect the tips of fingers and toes. Although nails are non-living structures, they require proper cleansing and care to maintain their strength, appearance, and health. Poor nail care can lead to brittleness, discoloration, breakage, and fungal infections.

---

# Characteristics of Nails

- Composed of hard keratin.
    
- Grow from the nail matrix.
    
- Fingernails grow about 3 mm/month.
    
- Constant exposure to water, detergents, and chemicals.
    
- Prone to brittleness and fungal infections.
    

---

# Cleansing Needs of Nails

## 1. Removal of Dirt and Microorganisms

- Nails easily accumulate dirt and microbes.
    
- Regular cleaning prevents infections.
    

## 2. Proper Nail Hygiene

- Keep nails short and clean.
    
- Clean underneath the free edge of nails.
    

## 3. Removal of Nail Cosmetics

- Nail polish should be removed periodically.
    
- Use non-acetone nail polish removers to prevent excessive drying.
    

---

# Care Needs of Nails

## 1. Moisturization

Frequent contact with water causes dehydration of the nail plate.

### Benefits

- Prevents brittleness.
    
- Maintains flexibility.
    
- Reduces splitting.
    

### Ingredients

- Jojoba oil
    
- Cuticle oil
    
- Glycerin
    
- Vitamin E
    

---

## 2. Cuticle Care

### Importance

- Cuticle protects the nail matrix from infection.
    

### Care

- Keep cuticles moisturized.
    
- Avoid aggressive cutting of cuticles.
    

---

## 3. Prevention of Brittle Nails

### Causes

- Repeated wetting and drying.
    
- Excessive use of detergents.
    
- Nutritional deficiencies.
    

### Care

- Apply nail oils regularly.
    
- Wear gloves during household work.
    

---

## 4. Prevention of Fungal Infections (Onychomycosis)

### Causes

- Moist environment.
    
- Poor hygiene.
    

### Care

- Keep nails clean and dry.
    
- Avoid sharing nail instruments.
    

---

## 5. Prevention of Nail Staining

### Causes

- Dark-colored nail polish.
    
- Chemicals.
    

### Care

- Use a base coat before nail polish.
    
- Remove polish regularly.
    

---

# Common Nail Problems

|Problem|Cause|
|---|---|
|Brittle nails|Repeated hydration-dehydration|
|Nail splitting|Dryness and trauma|
|Fungal infection|Moist environment|
|Nail staining|Nail polish pigments|
|Cuticle damage|Excessive trimming|

# Conclusion

Proper nail care includes **regular cleansing, moisturization, cuticle protection, prevention of fungal infections, and avoidance of excessive chemical exposure**. Regular use of nail oils and maintaining nail hygiene help keep nails healthy, strong, and attractive.
