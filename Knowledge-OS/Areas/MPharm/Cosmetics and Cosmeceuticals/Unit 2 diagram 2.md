For exam purposes, draw **simple labeled diagrams**. Examiners usually give marks for neatness and relevance, not artistic quality.

# Highest-Scoring Trick

For **Face, Eyelids, Lips, Hands, Feet, Neck, Body, Underarm**, draw:

```text
      BODY PART
          ↓
     Cleansing
          ↓
       Care
          ↓
 Healthy Skin
```

For **Nails** draw nail structure.

For **Scalp** draw hair follicle with sebaceous gland.

These are the quickest diagrams to draw in under **30 seconds each** and still earn diagram marks in B.Pharm/M.Pharm cosmetic exams.
