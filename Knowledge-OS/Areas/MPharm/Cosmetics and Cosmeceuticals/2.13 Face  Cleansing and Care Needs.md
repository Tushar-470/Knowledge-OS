# Face: Cleansing and Care Needs (10 Marks)

## Introduction

The face is the most exposed part of the body and is continuously subjected to **dust, pollutants, UV radiation, cosmetics, and excess sebum production**. Therefore, proper cleansing and care are essential to maintain healthy skin and prevent acne, dryness, pigmentation, and premature aging.

---

## Biological Features of Facial Skin

- High density of **sebaceous glands**.
    
- Continuous exposure to sunlight and pollution.
    
- T-zone (forehead, nose, chin) is usually oily.
    
- Cheeks and periorbital area may be dry and sensitive.
    

These characteristics make facial care different from other body areas.

---

# Cleansing Needs of Face

### 1. Removal of Sebum

- Excess sebum can clog pores and cause acne.
    
- Cleansers help remove excess oil.
    

### 2. Removal of Dirt and Pollutants

- Environmental pollutants accumulate on facial skin.
    
- Daily cleansing prevents skin damage.
    

### 3. Removal of Cosmetics

- Makeup residues should be removed completely.
    
- Prevents pore blockage and irritation.
    

### 4. Maintain Skin pH

- Ideal facial cleanser should have pH **5.0–5.5**.
    
- Preserves the acid mantle and skin barrier.
    

### Ideal Facial Cleansers

- Gel cleansers → Oily skin
    
- Cream cleansers → Dry skin
    
- Mild non-soap cleansers → Sensitive skin
    

---

# Care Needs of Face

## 1. Moisturization

Maintains hydration and prevents dryness.

### Benefits

- Reduces transepidermal water loss (TEWL)
    
- Maintains skin barrier
    
- Improves softness and elasticity
    

**Ingredients:** Glycerin, Hyaluronic Acid, Ceramides.

---

## 2. Sun Protection

The face receives maximum UV exposure.

### Importance

- Prevents sunburn
    
- Prevents pigmentation
    
- Delays wrinkles and photoaging
    
- Reduces risk of skin cancer
    

### Recommendation

- Broad-spectrum sunscreen
    
- SPF 30–50+
    

---

## 3. Acne Control

For oily and acne-prone skin:

- Use non-comedogenic products.
    
- Avoid excessive oil-based cosmetics.
    

**Ingredients:** Salicylic acid, Niacinamide.

---

## 4. Anti-Aging Care

Helps prevent wrinkles and fine lines.

**Ingredients:**

- Retinoids
    
- Vitamin C
    
- Peptides
    
- Antioxidants
    

---

# Conclusion

Facial skin requires **gentle cleansing, moisturization, sun protection, acne control, and anti-aging care**. Proper use of pH-balanced cleansers, moisturizers, and sunscreens helps maintain healthy, youthful, and protected skin.
