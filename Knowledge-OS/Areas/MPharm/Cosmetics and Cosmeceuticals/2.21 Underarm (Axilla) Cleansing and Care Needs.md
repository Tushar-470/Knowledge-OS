# Underarm (Axilla): Cleansing and Care Needs (10 Marks)

## Introduction

The **underarm (axilla)** is a unique body area containing a high number of **apocrine and eccrine sweat glands**. Due to sweating, hair growth, bacterial colonization, and friction, the underarm is prone to **body odor, excessive sweating, irritation, and hyperpigmentation**. Therefore, proper cleansing and care are essential.

---

# Characteristics of Underarm Skin

- High density of **apocrine sweat glands**.
    
- High density of **eccrine sweat glands**.
    
- Warm and moist environment.
    
- Rich bacterial flora.
    
- Frequent friction from clothing and shaving.
    
- Prone to odor and pigmentation.
    

---

# Cleansing Needs of Underarm

## 1. Removal of Sweat

- Sweat accumulates throughout the day.
    
- Regular cleansing prevents odor formation.
    

## 2. Removal of Bacteria

- Bacteria break down sweat and produce unpleasant odor.
    
- Proper cleansing reduces microbial growth.
    

## 3. Removal of Deodorant Residues

- Cleansing removes accumulated cosmetic residues and dirt.
    

## 4. Use of Mild Cleansers

- Avoid harsh alkaline soaps.
    
- Use gentle, fragrance-free cleansers when skin is sensitive.
    

### Recommended Cleansers

- Mild body wash
    
- Antibacterial cleansers
    
- Fragrance-free cleansers
    

---

# Care Needs of Underarm

## 1. Control of Body Odor

### Cause

- Bacterial decomposition of apocrine sweat.
    

### Care

- Use deodorants regularly.
    

### Ingredients

- Zinc ricinoleate
    
- Antimicrobial agents
    

---

## 2. Control of Excessive Sweating

### Care

- Use antiperspirants.
    

### Ingredients

- Aluminum salts
    
- Aluminum chlorohydrate
    

These temporarily block sweat ducts and reduce sweat production.

---

## 3. Prevention of Hyperpigmentation

### Causes

- Shaving
    
- Friction
    
- Irritation
    
- Inflammation
    

### Care

- Gentle shaving
    
- Use of skin-lightening agents
    

### Ingredients

- Niacinamide
    
- Kojic acid
    
- Alpha-arbutin
    
- Vitamin C
    

---

## 4. Prevention of Irritation

### Causes

- Deodorants
    
- Shaving
    
- Friction
    

### Care

- Use alcohol-free products.
    
- Moisturize after shaving.
    

---

## 5. Maintenance of Hygiene

- Daily cleansing.
    
- Regular hair removal if desired.
    
- Wearing clean, breathable clothing.
    

---

# Common Underarm Problems

|Problem|Cause|
|---|---|
|Body odor|Bacterial breakdown of sweat|
|Excessive sweating|Hyperactive sweat glands|
|Hyperpigmentation|Friction and irritation|
|Razor bumps|Improper shaving|
|Irritation|Harsh deodorants|

# Conclusion

The underarm requires **regular cleansing, odor control, sweat control, prevention of hyperpigmentation, and maintenance of hygiene**. Proper use of deodorants, antiperspirants, and gentle cleansing products helps keep the underarm healthy, fresh, and irritation-free.
