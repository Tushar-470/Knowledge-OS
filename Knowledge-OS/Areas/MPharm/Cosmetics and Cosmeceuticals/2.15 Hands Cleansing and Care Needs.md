# Hands: Cleansing and Care Needs (10 Marks)

## Introduction

The hands are continuously exposed to **water, soaps, detergents, chemicals, dust, sunlight, and microorganisms**. Frequent washing and environmental exposure can damage the skin barrier, causing dryness, irritation, and premature aging. Therefore, proper cleansing and care are essential for maintaining healthy hands.

---

# Characteristics of Hand Skin

- Frequently exposed to external environment.
    
- Fewer sebaceous glands than facial skin.
    
- Easily loses moisture.
    
- Prone to dryness, roughness, and contact dermatitis.
    
- Constant contact with microbes and chemicals.
    

---

# Cleansing Needs of Hands

## 1. Removal of Dirt and Microorganisms

- Hand washing removes dirt, dust, and microbes.
    
- Helps prevent infections and disease transmission.
    

## 2. Removal of Chemicals and Irritants

- Removes detergents, oils, and environmental contaminants.
    

## 3. Use of Mild Cleansers

- Harsh soaps can damage the skin barrier.
    
- Mild pH-balanced cleansers are preferred.
    

### Ideal Hand Cleansers

- Mild liquid soaps
    
- Syndet (soap-free) cleansers
    
- Moisturizing hand washes
    

---

# Care Needs of Hands

## 1. Moisturization

Frequent washing removes natural lipids.

### Benefits

- Prevents dryness and roughness.
    
- Restores skin barrier.
    
- Reduces cracking.
    

### Ingredients

- Glycerin
    
- Urea
    
- Ceramides
    
- Hyaluronic acid
    

---

## 2. Protection of Skin Barrier

Barrier repair helps prevent:

- Irritation
    
- Dermatitis
    
- Water loss
    

### Barrier-Protecting Agents

- Dimethicone
    
- Petrolatum
    
- Ceramides
    

---

## 3. Sun Protection

Hands are continuously exposed to sunlight.

### Importance

- Prevents pigmentation.
    
- Prevents age spots.
    
- Delays premature aging.
    

### Recommendation

- Sunscreen SPF 30 or higher.
    

---

## 4. Protection During Work

Use protective gloves while:

- Handling detergents
    
- Cleaning
    
- Gardening
    
- Industrial work
    

This reduces chemical damage and irritation.

---

# Common Problems of Hands

|Problem|Cause|
|---|---|
|Dryness|Frequent washing|
|Roughness|Moisture loss|
|Cracks/Fissures|Severe dryness|
|Contact Dermatitis|Chemicals and detergents|
|Pigmentation|UV exposure|

# Conclusion

Hand care involves **proper cleansing, moisturization, barrier protection, sun protection, and use of protective gloves**. Regular use of mild cleansers and moisturizing products helps maintain healthy, soft, and protected hands.
