# Computers in Market Analysis (10 Marks)

## Definition

**Market Analysis** is the process of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting market-related information to understand customer needs, market trends, competition, and business opportunities.

**Computers in Market Analysis** are used to collect, process, store, and analyze large amounts of pharmaceutical market data for effective decision-making.

---

# Introduction

The pharmaceutical industry operates in a highly competitive market. Companies need accurate information about:

- Customer needs
    
- Drug demand
    
- Competitors
    
- Pricing
    
- Market trends
    

Computers help analyze large datasets quickly and accurately, enabling better business decisions and strategic planning.

---

# Objectives of Market Analysis

1. Understand market demand.
    
2. Forecast future sales.
    
3. Analyze competitors.
    
4. Identify business opportunities.
    
5. Improve marketing strategies.
    
6. Increase profitability.
    

---

# Role of Computers in Market Analysis

## 1. Sales Forecasting

Computers use statistical models to predict future sales of pharmaceutical products.

### Benefits

- Production planning.
    
- Inventory management.
    
- Revenue prediction.
    

---

## 2. Market Trend Analysis

Computers analyze:

- Disease prevalence
    
- Prescription patterns
    
- Consumer preferences
    
- Healthcare trends
    

### Importance

Helps companies identify emerging opportunities.

---

## 3. Competitor Analysis

Computers collect and analyze information about:

- Competitor products
    
- Product launches
    
- Pricing strategies
    
- Market share
    

### Benefits

- Better competitive strategies.
    
- Improved product positioning.
    

---

## 4. Customer Analysis

Computers help understand:

### Physicians

- Prescribing behavior.
    
- Drug preferences.
    

### Patients

- Drug usage patterns.
    
- Treatment needs.
    

### Benefits

Improves customer satisfaction and marketing effectiveness.

---

## 5. Pharmacoeconomic Analysis

Computers evaluate:

### Cost-Benefit Analysis

Benefits compared with treatment cost.

### Cost-Effectiveness Analysis

Clinical outcomes relative to cost.

### Cost-Utility Analysis

Cost per quality-adjusted life year (QALY).

### Importance

Supports healthcare and pricing decisions.

---

## 6. Pricing Analysis

Computers help determine:

- Product pricing.
    
- Profit margins.
    
- Market competitiveness.
    

### Benefits

- Better pricing strategy.
    
- Improved profitability.
    

---

## 7. Database Management

Computers maintain databases containing:

- Sales records
    
- Customer information
    
- Prescription data
    
- Market research reports
    

### Advantages

- Easy retrieval.
    
- Accurate record keeping.
    

---

## 8. Business Intelligence (BI)

Modern pharmaceutical companies use BI tools to convert raw data into useful information.

### Examples

- Power BI
    
- Tableau
    
- SAP
    
- Oracle Analytics
    

### Benefits

- Real-time decision making.
    
- Better visualization of trends.
