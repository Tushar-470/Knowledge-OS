# Naming Standards

## Current Evaluation

Subject names are academically meaningful and should be preserved. Numbered note titles should not be renamed or renumbered because they encode syllabus order and exam flow.

## Recommended Standards

- Preserve numbered academic notes exactly unless a future rename is necessary for Git/path safety.
- Keep subject folders separate.
- Use `Dashboard.md` inside folders where folder context makes the title clear.
- Use explicit root-level dashboards such as `EXAM_DASHBOARD.md` and `THESIS_DASHBOARD.md` for cross-area control pages.
- For future research papers, prefer short citation-key filenames such as `AuthorYear Short Topic.md`, while keeping full citation details inside the note.

## Filename Safety

Very long filenames can cause Windows, Git, and sync issues. If shortening is needed later, preserve the numbering prefix and full academic title inside the note body.
